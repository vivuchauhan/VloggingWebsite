Moila Top


Moila Top or also known as Moila Bugyal is a Himalayan Alpine Meadow near Chakrata which is roughly 40km from this location. This meadow has scenic views throughout with panoramic views of the Himalayas at the top. To reach this location one has to cross the mystical Deodar Forest. There are few caves at the corner of the Bugyal that are believed to be built by Pandavas, there is also a Shiva temple at the top.



This scenic location has a high biodiversity with many indigenous species living in this ecosystem. During the winter times, this place receives snowfall, not only that but snow can be found here in other seasons as well.



Head out on this 10.5-km out-and-back trail near Shillai, Uttarakhand. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 3 h 45 min to complete. This is a popular trail for birding, hiking, and snowshoeing, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day.
